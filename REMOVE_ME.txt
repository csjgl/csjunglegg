The following files and folders are unneeded and can be safely deleted:

- api/auth/ (empty folder)
- server.js (not used in Vercel serverless setup)
- dist/ (build output, not needed in repo)
- .vite/ (Vite cache, not needed in repo)
- .vercel/cache/ (Vercel cache, not needed in repo)
- node_modules/ (dependencies, not needed in repo)

You can delete these using File Explorer or with these PowerShell commands:

Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\api\auth
Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\server.js
Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\dist
Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\.vite
Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\.vercel\cache
Remove-Item -Recurse -Force .\node_modules

Your source code and deployment will not be affected by removing these files/folders.
